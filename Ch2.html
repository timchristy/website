Ch. 2 Problem 1

Find a unit vector $\textbf{\hat{n}}$ normal to each of the following surfaces.

(a)\hspace{2mm} z = 2 - x - y\\ (b)\hspace{2mm} z = (x^2+y^2)^{1/2}

Recall that the normal vector, $\textbf{\hat{n}}$, for $z = f(x,y)$ is

$\textbf{\hat{n}} = \frac{-\hat{i}\frac{\partialf}{\partialx} -\hat{j}\frac{\partialf}{\partialy} + \hat{k}}{\sqrt{\left(\frac{\partialf}{\partialx}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{\partialf}{\partialy}\right)^2 + 1}$
